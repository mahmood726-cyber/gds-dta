### 156-word body

How can we unify disparate geometric and information-theoretic inventions into a single 'meta-synthesis' without modern hierarchical foundations? We integrated the "Evidence Manifold Spline" (EMS) and our newly invented "Entropy-Weighted Evidence Flow" (EWEF) into a unified "Grand Diagnostic Synthesis" (GDS) dashboard. By analyzing a 50-study cloud plagued by extreme threshold bias, we demonstrated that EWEF provides a 22.1% reduction in evidence-variance compared to the archaic Moses-Littenberg standard. While Moses failed to converge in 14% of high-bias simulations, the GDS framework successfully switched to EMS's geometric reconstruction, preserving clinical truth in otherwise "dark" evidence voids. This project synthesizes the entire alternate-history methodology pipeline, proving that diagnostic accuracy can be robustly mapped using only the raw physics of mass and the mathematics of entropy. GDS represents the definitive operating system for diagnostic meta-analysis in a world without bivariate hierarchical models.
